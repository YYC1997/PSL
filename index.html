- ABOUT -: The PSL Student Board is a pan-Philadelphia organization dedicated to connecting students to entrepreneurship-oriented opportunities, especially those within the Philly startup ecosystem. Led by student representatives from schools all across the city, the Board and its members provide resources like online guides and podcasts, hosts events like [INSERT FUTURE EVENTS], and helps students find the right path to help develop their interests in various aspects of entrepreneurship and startups. Our mission is to make it easier for students to explore startups, one of the most exciting sectors of the 21st century job market, and start, grow, and build a company or a career in one of the best cities in the country, Philadelphia!
